Highlights

Grow an idea from small to big.

Starting from a Block, to a cluster of Blocks, to a Canvas, then to nested Canvases.

Click the top-right "expand" icon on a Block to open it as a Canvas.

Remix ideas to connect the dots.

Reusing existing ideas in multiple Canvases.

Search the ideas to reuse, and drag them out to the Canvas.

The Block that you drag out from the search tool is a "reference" to the idea you want to reuse. So when you edit an idea from a reference, all other references to that idea reflect the changes.

Recent, Backlinks, Slash Commands.

Recent Tool: Navigate between recently visited Concepts.

Backlink Tool (the one on the bottom-left corner): Find out what concepts embed current concept, so that you never lose context when going into details.
